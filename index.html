How To Order

I happily take custom Sweet Fresh Products orders for birthdays, weddings and every special occasion!

In the first instance, please complete the Contact Form. It really helps if you can give me the exact date of your event so I can check availability up front. I will then contact you by email to discuss your ideas and give you a price.

Then, if you would like to book, I ask for a 50% retainer deposit which is payable either by PayPal, check or cash. I will send an invoice so you always have a record of your payments.
